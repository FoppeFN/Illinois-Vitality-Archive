Our Mission

Who We Are

The Illinois Vitality Archive (IVA) is a project developed by students at Southern Illinois University Edwardsville (SIUE) to preserve and provide access to vital records — including birth, death, and marriage records — from counties across the state of Illinois.

What We Do

We aim to build an easy-to-use, searchable database of Illinois vital records. Whether you are researching your family history, verifying historical data, or exploring genealogical connections, IVA is designed to make that process as straightforward as possible.

Our Goals

  • Provide a clean, intuitive search experience for birth, death, and marriage records.
  • Cover all counties in Illinois with accurate and well-organized data.
  • Make historical records accessible to the public in a respectful and responsible way.
  • Deliver a platform that can be handed off and expanded with real records in the future.

The Team

IVA was built by a team of SIUE computer science students: Nick Foppe, Marquis Pritchett, Ian Dunn, and Zachariah Brinken — in collaboration with our project sponsor.
